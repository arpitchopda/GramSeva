About Us

Gram Seva is dedicated to empowering rural India by connecting villagers to government schemes and facilities. Our mission is to bridge the gap between rural communities and the resources they need to build a better future. We aim to provide easy access to information, streamline application processes, and ensure transparency in availing government benefits.
