Education

The Maharashtra Government has launched multiple education schemes to enhance literacy rates, promote higher education, and ensure inclusive access to quality education across all sections of society.

MahaDBT Scholarship

Direct benefit transfer scholarships for students of backward classes.

Important Dates: Application starts in August every year.

Right to Education (RTE) Admissions

25% reservation in private schools for students from weaker sections.

Important Dates: Application opens around March-April.

Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Vasatigruh Nirvah Bhatta Yojna

Hostel allowance for students of professional and technical courses.

Important Dates: Open throughout academic session.

Alternate schooling option for students who drop out due to various reasons.
