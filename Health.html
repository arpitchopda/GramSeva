Health Schemes

Maharashtra government has launched various health-related schemes to improve healthcare access and affordability for all citizens, especially for rural and underprivileged populations.

Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana

Cashless treatment up to Rs. 1.5 lakh for families below poverty line.

Important Dates: Open all year

Apply

Rajiv Gandhi Jeevandayee Arogya Yojana

Provides medical care for critical illness to low-income families.

Important Dates: Ongoing

Apply

Balasaheb Thackeray Accidental Insurance Scheme

Financial assistance in case of accidental death or disability.

Important Dates: Immediate registration required

Apply

Free Dialysis Scheme

Free dialysis service for poor patients at empanelled hospitals.

Important Dates: Hospital registration required

Apply

Health Check-Up Scheme for Women

Free yearly health checkups for women aged 18 to 60.

Important Dates: Yearly rounds
