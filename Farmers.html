Farmer Welfare Schemes

Maharashtra Government provides various support schemes for farmers including financial assistance, crop insurance, irrigation, and subsidies. These schemes aim to uplift the agricultural community and ensure sustainable farming practices.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Provides ₹6,000 annually to eligible farmers in three equal installments.

Ongoing – Paid every 4 months

Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Shetkari Karjmukti Yojana

Loan waiver scheme for marginal and small farmers with overdue crop loans.

Based on eligibility during financial year

Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme to protect against yield loss due to natural calamities.

Seasonal – Based on sowing season

Shiv Bhojan Yojana for Farmers

Subsidized meal scheme for farmers during distress or emergency situations.

Baliraja Chetana Abhiyan

Financial and psychological support for farmers in drought-prone regions.

Implemented in selected talukas annually

Gopinath Munde Shetkari Apghat Vima Yojana

Provides compensation to families of farmers who die in accidents.

Claims must be submitted within 6 months of incident

Helps farmers understand the nutrient status of their land and apply balanced fertilizers.
