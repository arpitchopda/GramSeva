Women Empowerment

The Government of Maharashtra has launched various schemes aimed at empowering women in education, employment, healthcare, self-employment, and safety. These initiatives strive to promote gender equality and uplift women from all sections of society.

1. Tejaswini Maharashtra

This program empowers adolescent girls and young women by providing vocational skills and life skills training.

Important Dates: Ongoing

2. Majhi Kanya Bhagyashree Yojana

Financial support for the girl child to encourage education and improve the gender ratio.

Important Dates: Enroll at birth or school admission

Apply

3. Udyogini Scheme

Offers financial assistance and loans to women entrepreneurs for starting businesses.

Important Dates: Based on bank schedules
